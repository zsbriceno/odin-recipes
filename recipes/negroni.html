Negroni Recipe

Description

The Negroni is a delicious cocktail that originates from Italy. It is a well-balanced cocktail, with hints of sweetness, bitterness, and juniper.

Ingredients

  • 1.25 oz London Dry Gin
  • 1 oz Campari, or any similar red bitter liqueur
  • 1 oz sweet red vermouth
  • Hand-full of small cubed ice
  • 1 slice of orange rind for garnish
  • 1 large ice cube

Steps

  1. Using a cocktail jigger, pour the necessary amounts of each liquid ingredient in a mixing glass.
  2. Pour the hand-full of small cubed ice into the mixing glass.
  3. Stir the liquid and ice about 10-15 times to properly mix the ingredients and properly dilute the cocktail.
  4. Place the large ice cube into the cocktail glass
  5. Using a strainer, pour the properly stirred and diluted cocktail into your cocktail glass
  6. Take the freshly peeled orange rind, squeeze above the cocktail to express the oils from the rind. Then place the rind in the cocktail for garnish
  7. Finally, take a sip, and enjoy!
