French 75 Recipe

Description

The French 75 is a refreshing cocktail that is often enjoyed on celebratory occasions.

Ingredients

  • 2 oz of London Dry Gin
  • 3/4 oz of freshly squeezed lemon juice
  • 1/2 oz of simple syrup
  • 1 oz of Brut champagne or similar dry sparkling wine
  • 1 handful of small cubed ice
  • 1 slice of lemon rind for garnish

Steps

  1. Using a cocktail jigger, pour the necessary amounts of gin, lemon juice, and simple syrup into a cocktail shaker.
  2. Pour the hand-full of cubed ice in the cocktail shaker along with the rest of the liquids
  3. Shake the cocktail shaker vigorously for 10-15 seconds
  4. Strain the contents into a flute-style glass
  5. Garnish the drink with the slice of lemon rind
  6. Lastly, enjoy! But be careful, as this drink packs a boozy punch.
